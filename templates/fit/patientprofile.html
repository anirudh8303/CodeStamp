Healthy Heart Rate
Your resting heart rate is your pulse when you are calmly sitting or lying. It’s best to measure your resting heart rate it in the morning before you get out of bed, according to the AHA. For adults 18 and older, a normal resting heart rate is between 60 and 100 beats per minute (bpm), depending on the person’s physical condition and age.For children ages 6 to 15, the normal resting heart rate isbetween 70 and 100 bpm, according to the AHA.

But a heart rate lower than 60 doesn’t necessarily mean you have a medical problem. Active people often have lower heart rates because their heart muscles don't need to work as hard to maintain a steady beat. Athletes and people who are very fit can have resting heat rate of 40 bpm.
Healthy Diet Tips

Healthy Eating Plan

A healthy eating plan gives your body the nutrients it needs every day while staying within your daily calorie goal for weight loss. A healthy eating plan also will lower your risk for heart disease and other health conditions.

A healthy eating plan:

  • 1. Emphasizes vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and fat-free or low-fat dairy products
  • 2 Includes lean meats, poultry, fish, beans, eggs, and nuts
  • 3. Limits saturated and trans fats, sodium, and added sugars
  • 4. Controls portion sizes
