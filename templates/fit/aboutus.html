About Amycare

Amycare connects patients to doctors letting them book an appointment with them and then leading them to their nearby pharmacists for the medicines prescribed by the doctor.

How is it Helpful?

It helps the patient to share his/her medical history with thw doctor and the previous medications digitally. It also connects to the nearby chemist for easy availability of right medicines.
