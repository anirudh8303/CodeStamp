About Amycare

A patient-centered information exchange system where the patients, doctors and the pharmacists are all connected under one roof.

How is it Helpful?

It helps the patient to share his/her medical history with the doctor and the previous medications digitally. It also connects to the nearby chemist for easy availability of right medicines.
